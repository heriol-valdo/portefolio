ReLiveMe

Is a structure which exercises in the writing, publication of books, accompanies you in your steps of memories going from the idea to the concretization. Founded in 2022 by Mr Heriol Zeufack, it aims to introduce young people, passionate or not, to the benefits of reading, offering them the possibility thanks to tailor-made advice to bring their dreams to life through memorial writing. Following a method and a schedule adapted to different people.
Concerned about the gaze brought to her and given the technological advancement she offers outside the world of books services in various fields, notably that of computing.
